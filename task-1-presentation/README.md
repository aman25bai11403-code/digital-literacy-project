Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Overview
As the Student Digital Ambassador, I have created this infographic to help my batchmates understand the fundamentals of Digital Literacy. The goal is to encourage a critical mindset, safe browsing habits, and professional communication in an academic environment.

Design Details
Tool Used: Canva

Key Topics Covered:

Definition of Digital Literacy: Moving beyond basic tech skills to critical evaluation of information.

Safe Internet Habits: Focus on verification (SIFT method), privacy (2FA), and cybersecurity awareness.

Email Etiquette: Best practices for subject lines, greetings, and professional sign-offs.

Project Report
For this task, I used Canva to design a clean, high-impact infographic titled "Level Up Your Digital Literacy." I chose this tool because its extensive library of vector graphics and typography allowed me to create a professional resource that aligns with modern design standards, ensuring the information is accessible to my batchmates.

The design is structured into three clear pillars: an introductory definition that frames digital literacy as a critical mindset, a checklist for "Safe Internet Habits" focusing on verification and security, and a "Golden Rules" section for professional email etiquette. I utilized a high-contrast burgundy and cream color scheme to create a sense of academic authority while keeping the tone friendly and engaging.

One thing I found particularly interesting was the challenge of visual hierarchy. I initially had trouble balancing the large headline with the body text, but I discovered that using rounded content blocks helped guide the eye naturally from top to bottom. It was a great exercise in learning how to simplify complex concepts into "snackable" bullet points without losing the core message.

Task 2: Build Your Student Digital Portfolio

For this module, I set up and updated profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Stack Overflow. These platforms were chosen because they are essential for a modern technical career, covering code transparency, professional networking, and community contribution.

GitHub serves as my digital laboratory for hosting projects. LinkedIn acts as my formal professional identity where I have updated my current degree and institution details. Stack Overflow allows me to learn from and eventually contribute to the global developer community.

In the coming four years, I plan to use GitHub as a living archive of my semester projects. I will use LinkedIn to document my milestones as a Student Digital Ambassador and to seek internship opportunities. These platforms will ensure I have a credible digital footprint by the time I graduate.

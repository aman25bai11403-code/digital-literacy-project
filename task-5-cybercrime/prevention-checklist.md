**Stay Safe Online: Prevention Checklist**

- **Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money**; a PIN is only required for sending money.
- **Verify the sender's identity** before scanning any QR codes sent via WhatsApp or social media.
- **Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** on all social media and email accounts.
- **Use strong, unique passwords** for different platforms and change them regularly.
- **Avoid clicking on suspicious links** in SMS or emails that claim you have won a prize.
- **Keep your banking and mobile apps updated** to the latest security versions.
- **Do not share your OTP** (One Time Password) with anyone, including people claiming to be bank officials.
- **Regularly monitor your bank statements** for any unauthorized small transactions.

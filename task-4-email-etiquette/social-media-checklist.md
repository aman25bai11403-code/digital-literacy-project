**🛡️ Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students**

**The Do's (Best Practices):**

- **Do** set your profiles to private to control who can see your personal information and photos.
- **Do** use a professional headshot and a clean bio on platforms like LinkedIn to attract recruiters.
- **Do** think twice before posting; once something is online, it can be permanent even if deleted.
- **Do** engage in positive digital citizenship by supporting peers and sharing educational content.
- **Do** verify information from a trusted source before resharing to prevent the spread of misinformation.

**The Don'ts (Risky Behaviors):**

- **Don't** post confidential information such as your registration number, home address, or phone number.
- **Don't** engage in "cyber-venting" or posting negative comments about professors or institutions.
- **Don't** use unprofessional language or slang in your public bios and comments.
- **Don't** accept follow requests from completely unknown or suspicious accounts.
- **Don't** post photos or videos that depict illegal activities or violate college code-of-conduct
